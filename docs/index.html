rs-manager

Stats Manager for Rocksmith Remastered 2014

Rocksmith is great but its UI leaves a lot to be desired especially when you have a lot of songs in your collection. This app uses Rocksmith + Steam data to consolidate stats, mastery, dlc and song lists under one roof.

Features

Stats

  • Track your mastery/completion/score attack stats of your entire collection and setlists
  • Track your per-song stats viz. mastery, progress history .

Steam

  • integrates with steam, search dlcs and track your purchased stats.
  • sort dlcs by acquired date
  • discover similar dlcs based on genre

Setlists

  • unlimited setlists to sort your collection
  • add songs manually or generate setlists based on filters
  • import and export to Rocksmith song list (integrates rsrtools)

Realtime Stats (integrates RockSniffer)

  • track note hit on a per phrase level
  • track perfect hits on a per phrase level (score attack)
  • record raw audio
